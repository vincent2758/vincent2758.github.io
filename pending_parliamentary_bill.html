Ban on western-influenced media from affecting our children

Presented by: The President Hruqwer of Minister of Education.

STATUS: PENDING BEFORE PARLIAMENTARY CONFERENCE CALL.

Preamble

This Bill seeks to not let teenagers in school to be exposed to westernised influencers cultures. For example: the usage of term "YOLO", the idea of Atheism, the idea of LGBT which challenge the true nature of human attraction, and so on.

Section 1 – Purpose

The purpose of this Bill is to ensure the nation's education quality to be always maintained at the highest standard through systematic reforms, enhanced teacher training, and improved learning resources for all students.

Section 2 – Implementation

Upon approval, the Ministry of Education shall begin to ban phones and other electronic gadgets from entring school. (If it is necessary to bring, for example, Basic Science Computer subject requiring students to bring their laptop, then it is permissble to do so.)
