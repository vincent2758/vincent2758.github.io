National Defense Expansion Bill

Presented by: The Right Honourable Members of the House of Commons

STATUS: PENDING BEFORE PARLIAMENT

Preamble

This Bill seeks to expand the scope of national defense readiness through allocation of additional resources to the armed forces and modernization of defense infrastructure.

Section 1 – Purpose

The purpose of this Bill is to ensure the nation's defense preparedness by strengthening military capacity and enhancing technological advancement in security systems.

Section 2 – Implementation

Upon approval, the Ministry of Defense shall begin assessment of current infrastructure and allocate funds accordingly under the Defense Modernization Act 2025.
